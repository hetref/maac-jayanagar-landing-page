Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this for?

✅ Beginners eager to explore 3D design, animation, and Gen AI-enhanced creative workflows

✅ Students and hobbyists looking to level up their skills using the latest AI tools

✅ Professionals switching to creative tech or expanding their skillset with Gen AI integrations

What is the Bachelor of Vocational Studies in Animation program?
It’s a comprehensive degree program combining creative animation principles with vocational skills, including hands-on training in 3D animation, VFX, game design, and AI-powered digital media creation.
What if I have no experience?
No problem at all! Our course is designed for complete beginners. You'll start with the basics and gradually move to advanced techniques with guided support.
What career opportunities does the degree open up?
Graduates can pursue careers as animators, VFX artists, game designers, digital media creators, and AI animation specialists in film, gaming, advertising, and architectural visualization industries.
Who will be teaching this course?
The course is led by certified instructors and professional animators with years of experience in the industry. You'll also get mentorship throughout your learning journey.
What will I get after completing the course?
You'll build a strong portfolio, receive a certificate of completion, and get exclusive access to job-ready resources and placement assistance.
